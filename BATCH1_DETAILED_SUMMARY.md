| ENV-02 | 低氣壓 | 待提取 | 待提取 |
| THERM-01 | 熱濫用 | 待提取 | 待提取 |

**豁免條款（6 項）：**
1. **not applicable to test**（4 項相同）
   - 內容：`The requirement relating to voltage is not applicable to test cells and batteries at fully discharged states.`
   - 意義：完全放電狀態的電池，電壓要求不適用

2. **unless detonation**
   - 內容：`The test is performed twice unless detonation of an acceptor is observed earlier.`
   - 意義：若提前觀察到爆炸，測試停止

3. **except**
   - 內容：`loss of material (except battery casing, handling devices or labels)`
   - 意義：電池外殼、把手、標籤的損失不計入質量損失

**備註：** UN38.3 的豁免條件主要與測試狀態（完全放電）和測試終止條件（爆炸）相關。
